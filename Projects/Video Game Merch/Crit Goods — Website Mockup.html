03 · Why Crit Goods

Built for fans.
Made to last.

01 / 03

Designed by players

Every drop starts with a fan question: "why doesn't this exist yet?" Our designers play the games before they design for them. We don't ship generic-gamer-logo SKUs. We ship things we'd wear ourselves.

02 / 03

Nothing sells out

We print on demand at partner facilities. That means no waitlists, no "restocking soon," no landfill. If you see it, you can order it — even at 2am after a boss drop.

03 / 03

Made to outlive the game

240gsm+ cotton. 300gsm uncoated matte paper. Water-based inks that don't crack after wash ten. Pins with real die-struck weight. Price up so the quality can stay up.
