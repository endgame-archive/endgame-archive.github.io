## HIGHLIGHT COVER SYSTEM

Each Highlight uses the Prism Diamond as the base icon.

| Highlight | Icon Color | Content |
|---|---|---|
| MANIFESTO | Pearl White | Brand story, Manifesto Card, founder note |
| REVEALS | UV Purple | Customer setups, community sanctuaries |
| ARCHIVE | Chrome Silver | Product teasers, Archive No. series |
| STUDIO | Iridescent | BTS Huntsville lab, process videos |
| REGISTRY | Pearl + Gold | How to join, drop dates, countdown |

**Cover template (for each):**
- Background: Pearl white (MANIFESTO, REGISTRY) or Dark/UV (ARCHIVE, REVEALS) or Iridescent (STUDIO)
- Center icon: Prism Diamond glyph
- Label: Highlight name in Space Mono below icon
