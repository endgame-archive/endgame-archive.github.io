## COLOR & LIGHTING MANIFESTO

### The "Pearl" Base
- High exposure, low contrast
- +10 Tint (Pink/Purple side)

### The "Ooze" Pop
- Saturation +20, Vibrance +30 (Cyans and Violets only)

### Lighting Direction
- "Raking Light" — single light source from the side
- Catches the Liquid Chrome reflections
- Avoid flat front-on lighting

### The Aura Glow
- Soft "Dream Filter" / Bloom on all light sources
- Mimics the pearl-shimmer overlay
